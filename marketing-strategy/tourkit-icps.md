# TourKit Ideal Customer Profiles

## Primary ICP: Frontend Lead / Senior React Developer (50% of effort)

**Who:** Senior FE Engineer, Frontend Lead, Staff Engineer. Age 27-38, 5-12 years experience. Owns library selection at Series A-C SaaS (20-200 employees, $1M-$30M ARR, 3-15 person eng team).

**Stack:** React 18/19 + Next.js 14/15 App Router + TypeScript strict + Tailwind + shadcn/ui + Zustand/Jotai + pnpm/bun + Vercel/AWS

**Top 3 Pain Points:**
1. React Joyride broken on React 19, inline styles clash with Tailwind
2. SaaS tools ($249-$879/mo) too expensive for what they deliver, zero developer control
3. No headless option exists — every library forces its styling opinions

**Buying Triggers:** New product launch, activation metrics dropping, React 19 migration breaks Joyride, SaaS cost-cutting, switching to shadcn/ui, accessibility audit failure

**Decision:** Evaluates on GitHub in <5 min (README, stars, TS quality). Tries in 15 min. Ships basic tour week 1. Buys Pro week 2-8 ($99 = impulse corporate card, no procurement).

**Channels:** Twitter/X, r/reactjs, HN, This Week in React, GitHub Trending, Reactiflux/shadcn Discord

**Key Message:** "Headless product tours for React — hooks-based, accessible, built for the shadcn/ui era."

## Secondary ICP: Solo Founder / Indie Hacker (30% of effort)

**Who:** Building SaaS solo or with 1-2 co-founders. Full-stack, chose React/Next.js. Revenue $0-$30K MRR. Bootstrapped or lightly funded. Age 24-40.

**Stack:** "The 2026 indie stack" — Next.js 14/15, TypeScript, Tailwind + shadcn/ui, Prisma/Drizzle, Vercel, Stripe, Better Auth/Clerk

**Top 3 Pain Points:**
1. SaaS tour tools eat margins ($300/mo = 6% at $5K MRR)
2. Subscription fatigue — already paying for 10+ SaaS tools
3. Needs native shadcn/ui look, working in an afternoon

**Buying Triggers:** PH launch feedback says onboarding confusing, hit $1K MRR optimizing activation, saw peer tweet about tour-kit, evaluating one-time vs recurring costs

**Decision:** 0-15 min from discovery to working tour. Getting-started experience IS the sales pitch.

**Channels:** Indie hacker Twitter (@levelsio, @marc_louvion), Product Hunt, r/SaaS, r/indiehackers, YouTube (Marc Lou, Fireship)

**Key Message:** "Product tours for your Next.js SaaS. Free tier forever. Pro is $99 once — not $300/month."

## Tertiary ICP: Product Manager / Growth Lead (20% of effort)

**Who:** PM, Head of Product, Growth Lead at 50-500 employee company ($5M-$100M ARR). Non-technical or semi-technical. Currently managing tours via Appcues, Pendo, or UserGuiding.

**Current Spend:** $3,000-$48,000/year on product tour SaaS. Tour-kit Pro at $99 = 97-99% cost reduction.

**Top 3 Pain Points:**
1. SaaS costs escalating with MAU growth (5K to 50K MAUs = $249 to $879/mo without changing usage)
2. Vendor lock-in — switching means rebuilding everything
3. Need engineering help for SaaS tools anyway despite "no-code" promises

**Decision:** 6-8 week cycle. Discovers alternatives, builds eval doc, presents to eng lead (who becomes Primary ICP), eng spikes, PM builds business case with cost savings, finance approves.

**Channels:** Google Search, G2/Capterra, LinkedIn, Lenny's Newsletter, PLG Slack communities

**Key Message:** "Replace your $300/month Appcues subscription with a $99 one-time purchase your engineering team will enjoy using."

## Anti-Personas (Do NOT Target)

| Segment | Why Not | Redirect To |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Enterprise needing no-code builders (500+ employees) | Code-first by design, no visual editor | WalkMe, Pendo |
| Teams without React (Vue, Angular, Svelte) | Architecture is fundamentally React-native | Shepherd.js, Driver.js |
| Desktop app overlays (Electron) | Web DOM only | Native overlay solutions |
| SOC 2/HIPAA compliance buyers | No server to audit | Note: zero data leaves browser (privacy advantage) |
| Non-technical marketers without eng support | Requires React code | Appcues, UserGuiding |
