## Elevator Pitch (30 Seconds)

Tour-kit is a headless React library for product tours and onboarding. You write the UI with your own components -- shadcn/ui, Radix, whatever you use -- and tour-kit handles positioning, step sequencing, keyboard navigation, focus management, and accessibility. The core is free and MIT-licensed. Extended packages for analytics, checklists, announcements, and more cost $99 one-time. No subscriptions. No per-seat pricing. No iframe overlays that look nothing like your app.

---

## 6 Core Value Props

### 1. Headless & Composable
Your components. Your styles. Our engine. Core is hooks and utilities -- no DOM rendering, no CSS injection, no style conflicts.

### 2. shadcn/ui Native
First-class shadcn/ui support. Follows shadcn composition patterns, uses same Radix UI primitives.

### 3. $99 Once, Not $300/Month
Appcues: $2,988/yr. UserGuiding: $1,068/yr. Pendo: ~$6,000+/yr. Tour-kit Pro: $99 total.

### 4. TypeScript-First
Strict mode across all packages. Generic step types, discriminated unions, full inference. No `any` types.

### 5. Accessibility Built-In (WCAG 2.1 AA)
Focus traps, `aria-live` announcements, full keyboard nav, `prefers-reduced-motion` support. All default.

### 6. Beyond Tours
6 extended Pro packages: analytics (PostHog/Mixpanel/Amplitude), checklists, announcements, adoption tracking, media embeds (YouTube/Loom/Lottie), scheduling.
