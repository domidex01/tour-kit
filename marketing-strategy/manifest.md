## Quick Reference

- **Positioning:** "The open-source Appcues for React developers" / "Headless onboarding for React"
- **Pricing:** Free (MIT core) + $99 one-time Pro (analytics, checklists, announcements, adoption, media, scheduling)
- **Primary ICP:** Senior React developer / Frontend Lead at Series A-C SaaS (20-200 employees)
- **Key differentiators:** Headless, shadcn/ui native, TypeScript strict, WCAG 2.1 AA, $99 once vs $300/mo
- **Launch model:** Multiple launches (Supabase-style), not one big bang
