*Product Manager / Growth Lead. 20% of marketing effort.*

## Who

PM, Head of Product, or Growth Lead at a 50–500 employee company ($5M–$100M ARR). Non-technical or semi-technical. Currently managing tours via a SaaS platform (Appcues, Pendo, UserGuiding).

## Current spend

**$3,000–$48,000/year** on product tour SaaS. TourKit Pro at $99 = 97–99% cost reduction.

## Top pain points

1. SaaS costs escalating — MAU-based pricing grows with success (5K→50K MAUs = $249→$879/mo without changing usage)
2. Vendor lock-in — all tours live inside the vendor. Switching = rebuild everything.
3. Limited customization frustrates engineering
4. Performance impact — 50–200KB third-party scripts hurt Core Web Vitals
5. Need engineering help anyway despite "no-code" promises

## Decision process

**6–8 week cycle.** Longer than the other ICPs.

1. Discovers alternatives (Google, G2)
2. Builds internal eval doc
3. Presents to engineering lead (who becomes the [Primary ICP](frontend-lead.md))
4. Engineering spikes
5. PM builds business case with cost savings
6. Finance approves

## Key message

> "Replace your $300/month Appcues subscription with a $99 one-time purchase your engineering team will actually enjoy using."

## Channels

Google Search · G2 / Capterra · LinkedIn · Lenny's Newsletter · PLG Slack communities.

## What resonates

- Cost-savings math with their current vendor named
- Case studies from teams that migrated
- Business outcomes: activation rate uplift, onboarding completion
- Engineering team alignment (eng actually wants this, unlike their current SaaS)
- No-surprises pricing (no MAU tiers, no add-ons)

## Sales objections relevant to this ICP

See [audience/objections.md](objections.md). Most relevant:

- "We already use Appcues"
- "We lose the no-code editor"
- "Need enterprise features (SSO, audit)"

## Handoff note

This ICP's real buyer is the engineering lead. PM builds the business case; eng approves the tool. That means:

- Marketing content targeting this ICP must also include a technical credibility signal the eng lead will respect
- Landing pages should have a "forward to your engineering team" CTA
- Case studies should cite both a PM outcome (activation uplift) and an engineering outcome (bundle size, no vendor lock-in)
