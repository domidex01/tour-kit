## Position

- No React 19 support
- No TypeScript strictness
- No accessibility work
- No extended features
- No active maintenance

## TourKit wins

Every dimension. Reactour is a legacy option kept on life support by search rankings.

## How to position against it

- Don't attack abandoned projects publicly — unprofessional.
- In comparison pages, factual: "Last release: [date]. React 19: not supported."
- Migration guide: relatively simple because Reactour users often want TourKit's headless model anyway.
