## Key SEO insight

SaaS companies (UserGuiding, Chameleon, Whatfix, Userpilot) dominate every product tour keyword with blog content. **No open-source library has a meaningful content presence.** TourKit can be the first OSS project to invest seriously in developer-focused content marketing for this category.

High-value keyword gaps:

- "appcues alternative open source" — no actual OSS project ranks
- "react 19 product tour" — uncontested
- "headless product tour react" — no strong results
- "shadcn ui product tour" — minimal competition
- "react joyride alternative" — growing search volume as React 19 adoption accelerates

See [gtm/seo-content-strategy.md](../gtm/seo-content-strategy.md).
