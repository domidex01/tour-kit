## Position

Enterprise digital adoption platform. Deep integration with enterprise stacks, rich analytics, procurement-heavy sales cycle.

## Threat to TourKit

**Low** — different buyer, different category. WalkMe is for Fortune 500 with SOC 2/audit needs. TourKit is for React dev teams.

## When TourKit shows up in their orbit

- Startups outgrowing TourKit and considering enterprise DAP — acceptable exit path
- Teams inside enterprises that want a lightweight alternative for a single product (not the whole enterprise)
- Comparison searches ("walkme alternative") — low priority but worth a comparison page eventually

## How to position

- Don't compete on enterprise features (SSO, audit, compliance)
- Redirect: see [audience/anti-personas.md](../../audience/anti-personas.md)
- Pattern: "If you need SOC 2–audited DAP across your whole enterprise, WalkMe or Pendo is purpose-built for that. TourKit is for React teams that want to own their onboarding UI."
