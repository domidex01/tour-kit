## Stats

| | |
|---|---|
| Pricing | ~$4,000/mo avg (~$48K/yr) |
| Revenue | $300M (2025) |
| Customers | ~13,000 |
| Category | Enterprise DAP (guides + product analytics) |

## Strengths

- Full product analytics bundled with guides
- Mature enterprise platform
- Customer success integration
- Strong G2 / analyst presence

## Weaknesses

- **Enterprise pricing** — $4K/mo+
- Teams often overpay because they only need onboarding, not full analytics
- Heavy script (100KB+)
- Slow procurement process

## TourKit counter

- **Angle:** "$99 once vs $20K+/year" for teams that only need onboarding (not full product analytics)
- Not targeting Pendo's core buyer directly — but when the Product Manager ICP considers cheaper options, Pendo→TourKit is a legitimate path
- Pro tier's `@tour-kit/analytics` package (PostHog / Mixpanel / Amplitude integration) removes a reason to stay with Pendo for onboarding

## Threat to TourKit

**Low** — different buyer, different category. Not a direct competitor but shows up in comparison searches.
