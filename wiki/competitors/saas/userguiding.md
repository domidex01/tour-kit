## Stats

| | |
|---|---|
| Pricing | $174 – $499/mo |
| Positioning | Lower-entry alternative to Appcues |
| Notable | Aggressive SEO, AI Assistant feature, 30-day money-back |

## Strengths

- Lower entry price than Appcues
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- AI Assistant feature
- Strong SEO content strategy (ranks for many tour/onboarding keywords)

## Weaknesses

- Still MAU-based pricing
- Still limited customization vs code-first
- Still iframe / injected script approach
- Vendor lock-in

## TourKit counter

Same value prop as Appcues comparison — $99 once vs $2,088–$5,988/year. UserGuiding's SEO presence is actually useful: their comparison-page reader is primed to hear "here's the open-source alternative."

## How to position

- Comparison page priority: after Joyride, Appcues, Shepherd. Likely position #5.
- Framing matches Appcues: "Own the code, pay once, integrate natively"
- Borrow their SEO vocabulary on comparison pages to intercept traffic
