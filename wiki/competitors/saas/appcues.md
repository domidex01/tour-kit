*Primary SaaS comparison target. TourKit positions as "the open-source Appcues."*

## Stats

| | |
|---|---|
| Category | Low-Code SaaS |
| Essentials | $249/mo ($2,988/yr) |
| Growth | $879/mo ($10,548/yr) |
| Funding | $55M raised, ~$250–300M valuation (2022) |
| Founded | 2013 |

## Tier breakdown

- **Essentials ($249/mo):** Tours, tooltips, modals, NPS, basic analytics, 5 segments
- **Growth ($879/mo):** + Checklists, A/B testing, advanced analytics, unlimited segments

## Strengths

- No-code visual builder
- Mature platform, established brand
- A/B testing
- 14-day free trial
- Large content/SEO presence

## Weaknesses

- Expensive ($3K–$10K/year)
- MAU-based pricing — cost scales with success without added usage
- Limited customization vs code-first
- Vendor lock-in
- Performance overhead (~100–200KB of third-party JS)
- Can't match code-first design control

## TourKit counter

Same features across $99 Pro tier, at $99 once vs $3K–$10K/year. Your team already has React devs.

## 3-year cost comparison

| Tier | Year 1 | Year 3 |
|---|---|---|
| TourKit Pro | $99 | $99 |
| Appcues Essentials | $2,988 | $8,964 |
| Appcues Growth | $10,548 | $31,644 |

## How to position

- **Comparison page:** `/compare/tour-kit-vs-appcues` is a top-priority SEO page
- **Framing:** "The open-source Appcues" — use Cal.com / Calendly as the precedent
- **Audience:** Primary target is the [Product Manager ICP](../../audience/product-manager.md) currently paying for Appcues
- **Migration content:** "How we migrated from Appcues to TourKit and saved $8K/year" (case study format)

## Key SEO target

- "appcues alternative" — high intent
- "appcues alternative open source" — no current OSS ranker (uncontested)
- "appcues pricing" — long-tail, comparison angle
