| | |

| Pricing | $279 – $999/mo |
| Notable | Strong SEO content strategy |

## Position

Mid-market SaaS product adoption platform. Ranks well for "react product tour library" listicles.

## Strengths

- Strong SEO content
- Blog ranks for comparison/listicle keywords
- Decent UX for PMs

## Weaknesses

- Standard SaaS pricing issues (MAU-based, iframe, vendor lock-in)
- No code-first option

## TourKit counter

- **Content SEO is the primary battleground.** Chameleon ranks where TourKit wants to rank. Every TourKit comparison page is competing directly.
- Framing: standard SaaS-alternative angle — $99 once vs $3K–$12K/year

## How to position

- Comparison page priority: lower than Appcues/UserGuiding. Likely #6–7.
- Intercept their listicle traffic with comparison pages and tutorial content
