*What TourKit is, who it's for, and why it wins — in one page.*

## One-liner

**Headless product tours and onboarding for React.** The open-source Appcues for developers on the shadcn/ui stack. $99 once, not $300/month.

## What it is

A headless React library for product tours, onboarding flows, checklists, announcements, hints, and feature adoption. Hooks-based. TypeScript strict. WCAG 2.1 AA. Built natively for the shadcn/ui + Tailwind + Next.js stack.

- **Free tier (MIT):** `@tour-kit/core`, `@tour-kit/react`, `@tour-kit/hints` — covers 80% of use cases
- **Pro tier ($99 one-time, 5 sites, lifetime updates):** 7 additional packages — analytics, checklists, announcements, adoption, media, scheduling, surveys

See [product/tourkit.md](product/tourkit.md) and [product/packages.md](product/packages.md).

## Go-to-market in one paragraph

Launch Supabase-style: multiple launches, not one bang — core first, then each Pro package as its own PH/HN/Reddit moment. Documentation IS marketing; position against known incumbents ("open-source Appcues"); $99 vs $300/mo is category disruption, lead with it everywhere. SEO compound through comparison pages (Tour-Kit vs React Joyride, vs Appcues, vs Shepherd, etc.) — zero OSS competition on product tour keywords.
