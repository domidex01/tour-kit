## What's in each article

Each is ~5–10K words with:
- Executive summary
- Pricing analysis at scale (often including Vendr data, real invoice breakdowns)
- Feature comparison matrix
- Developer pain points ranked by evidence (G2, Capterra, Reddit mentions)
- Content gap analysis and keyword opportunities
- "When [Competitor] genuinely wins" section — intellectual honesty
- Recommended content angles for TourKit

## Workflow to use these

When asked to:

- **Update a competitor wiki page** — pull new facts (pricing, G2 scores, recent acquisitions, pain points) from the article, add to the page with the article as a source.
- **Draft a comparison page** — use the article as research backbone. Map to [content/comparison-template.md](comparison-template.md).
- **Generate a listicle / roundup** — aggregate "when they win" sections across multiple articles for fair coverage.
- **Research a blog topic** — the content-gap and keyword sections surface specific opportunities.
