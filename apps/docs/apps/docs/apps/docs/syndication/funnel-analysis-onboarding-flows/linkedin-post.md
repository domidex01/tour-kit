Most SaaS onboarding funnels track completion rate as the primary metric. That's the wrong number.

A user who clicks "Next" through every tooltip has 100% completion. A user who skips the tour, opens the dashboard, and creates their first project has 0% completion. The second user activated. The first one didn't.

Activation benchmarks vary more than most teams realize. AI/ML tools average 54.8%. FinTech sits at 5%. Using cross-industry averages as targets sends you chasing the wrong improvements.

One finding from the research that surprised me: no analytics tool can detect screen reader usage (intentionally, for privacy). If your onboarding breaks for assistive technology users, they drop off silently and your funnel data never shows it.
