## Title: CRM onboarding patterns: what HubSpot, Salesforce, and Zoho do differently

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/product-tours-crm-software-time-to-first-deal

## Comment to post immediately after:

I tore down onboarding flows across 6 CRM platforms (HubSpot, Salesforce, Freshsales, Zoho, Monday.com, Infusionsoft) and wrote up what actually reduces time to first deal — the core CRM onboarding metric.

The average sales rep takes 52 days to log their first deal in a new CRM. Structured onboarding cuts that by 40%. Three patterns appeared consistently: sample data injection to solve the empty-pipeline problem, role-based tour branching (SDR vs AE vs RevOps), and persistent checklists instead of one-shot walkthroughs.

The accessibility angle was the most surprising finding. CRM dashboards are keyboard-shortcut-heavy, and product tour overlays that trap focus break power-user workflows. I couldn't find a single competitor article addressing this failure mode.

The article includes React/TypeScript code examples for role-based routing and sample data hooks. Disclosure: I built Tour Kit (the library in the examples), but the CRM analysis and benchmarks are tool-agnostic.

Happy to discuss the patterns or the methodology.
