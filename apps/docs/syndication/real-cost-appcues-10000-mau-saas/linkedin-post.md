Appcues' pricing page says $249/month. At 10,000 MAU, the actual cost is $18,000–$24,000/year — before hidden fees.

I pulled real contract data from Vendr (127 Appcues purchases) and broke down every line item at the 10K MAU tier that most growth-stage SaaS companies hit.

The findings:

The Essentials plan locks out A/B testing, checklists, and Salesforce integration — forcing teams into the Growth tier at 253% more. Localization is gated entirely to Enterprise.

Hidden costs add up fast: MAU inflation from SDK placement on public pages, seat caps ($2,400/year to uncap beyond 10), analytics gaps that require running a second tool alongside.

Realistic Year 1 all-in: $23,100–$40,000.

The build-vs-buy framing is a false dichotomy. A third option — integrating an open-source library — costs roughly $33,000 over 3 years vs $57,000–$76,000 for Appcues. The gap widens every year.
