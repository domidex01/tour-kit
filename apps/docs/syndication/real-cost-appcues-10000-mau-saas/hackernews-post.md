## Title: The Real Cost of Appcues for a 10K MAU SaaS

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/real-cost-appcues-10000-mau-saas

## Comment to post immediately after:

I spent time researching onboarding tool pricing for a project I'm building, and the gap between Appcues' published pricing and the actual cost at 10K MAU was surprising enough to write up.

The key findings: Appcues' entry price is $249/month (2,500 MAU), but at 10,000 MAU on the Growth tier — the first plan that includes A/B testing and checklists — you're looking at $18,000-$24,000/year at list price. Vendr's data across 127 real contracts shows a median of $15,000/year with 21% average discount off list.

The pricing also scales non-linearly (doubling MAU costs more than double the price), and there are several costs not on the pricing page: forced tier upgrades on MAU overages, seat caps at 10 on the Growth plan ($2,400/year to uncap), localization locked entirely to Enterprise, and analytics gaps that typically require running a second tool alongside.

I also ran the math on the open-source library approach vs Appcues at this scale. Over 3 years at $150/hr senior dev rates: ~$33K for library integration + maintenance vs $57K-$76K for Appcues Growth.

Disclosure: I'm building Tour Kit, an open-source alternative, so I have obvious bias here. But every number comes from Appcues' own pricing page, Vendr marketplace data, and review aggregator sites — all linked in the article.
