**4/** The 60-second rule (from ProductLed):

Users need meaningful AI output within 60 seconds or they leave.

Most products stop at "suggest prompts." Winners actually pre-fill and RUN a prompt for the user.

**5/** Counterintuitive finding: when AI onboarding does too much, users never learn to prompt.

They hit success faster but don't build the skill. Good onboarding balances doing it for them with teaching them to do it.
