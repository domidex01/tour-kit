Most "What's New" modal tutorials stop at useState + a hardcoded list. That's about 30% of what a production implementation actually needs.

I wrote up the full approach: localStorage persistence for seen/unseen tracking, native HTML dialog for accessibility (0KB vs 188KB for react-modal), frequency rules, and analytics callbacks to measure which updates users actually engage with.

The interesting shift in the industry: teams are moving from tracking announcement open rates to measuring 7-day feature adoption rate. High impressions mean nothing if nobody tries the feature.
