Your onboarding tool has an exit cost. Have you calculated it?

SaaS price inflation hit 8.7% year-over-year in 2025, while IT budgets grew at 2.8%. For product tour tools like Appcues, Pendo, and Userpilot, that means your renewal is going up — and your tour definitions are stored in proprietary formats with no portable export.

I researched what migration actually costs:
- 3-4 weeks of engineering time
- 40-120 hours of flow recreation
- Historical analytics data: lost entirely
- Real cost: 2-3x the visible platform spend

74% of SaaS buyers now evaluate switching costs before purchase. But most evaluation frameworks focus on data portability and miss the bigger trap: logic portability. Your tour definitions encode product decisions — which elements to highlight, which segments see which flows. That's months of iteration you'd need to recreate from scratch.

I built an open-source alternative (Tour Kit), so my bias is clear. But the data on lock-in patterns applies regardless of what you switch to.
