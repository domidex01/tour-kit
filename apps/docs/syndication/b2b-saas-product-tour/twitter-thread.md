**3/** The single biggest lever: segment by role.

An admin's "aha moment" is connecting SSO.
An end-user's is completing a workflow.
A manager's is pulling a report.

One tour can't serve all three. Personalizing by role reduces time-to-aha by 40%.

**4/** Action-based tours > click-through tours.

Tours that require users to DO the thing (not just read about it) generate:
- 1.7x more signups
- 1.5x higher activation
- 80% more long-term customers
- 3x higher LTV
