# Product Tours for B2B SaaS: The Complete Playbook

Most B2B SaaS product tours chase completion rate. That's the wrong metric. Segmented onboarding lifts activation by 20-30% over generic feature tours, and role-targeted approaches push that to 30-50%. The difference between a tour users click through and one that actually changes behavior comes down to architecture decisions made before writing a single step.

This playbook covers role-based patterns, compliance requirements, and implementation code for B2B product tours that move activation metrics.

## Key findings

- Developer tools activate at 30-45%, CRM tools at 15-25%
- Role-based segmentation outperforms generic tours by 20-30%
- Four-step tours hit 60.1% completion; over five steps drops below 20%
- Action-based progression (requiring the task, not just clicks) produces 1.7x more signups
- WCAG 2.1 AA is legally required under the European Accessibility Act for EU-accessible SaaS
