Your onboarding completion rate is probably lying to you.

78% of users finish the tour. Half of them churn within a month. That's because "completed the tour" and "experienced product value" are two completely different things.

I dug into Time to Value (TTV) benchmarks from 547 SaaS companies. The data:

- Average TTV: 1 day 12 hours
- Top quartile: under 5 minutes
- Over 7 days: high churn risk
- 98% of users who don't hit value in 2 weeks are gone (Amplitude)

The most surprising finding? PLG and sales-led companies have nearly identical TTV. Self-serve isn't inherently faster. What matters is how intentionally you guide users to their first valuable action.
