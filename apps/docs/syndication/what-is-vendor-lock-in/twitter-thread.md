**1/** Switching onboarding tools costs 150-200% of your annual contract value. But the money isn't even the worst part.

**2/** The real cost is content migration. 40 tours, 15 checklists, 8 NPS surveys built over 2 years in a proprietary visual builder? You rebuild every one from scratch. There's no export path between Pendo, Appcues, or Userpilot.

**3/** Zluri identifies 4 types of SaaS lock-in: data, integration, process, contractual. Onboarding tools hit all four simultaneously. Process lock-in is the sneakiest — your team's knowledge is tied to one vendor's UI.

**4/** Some real examples: Pendo exports Avro files on 1-24 hour batch cadence. Appcues gates two-way integrations behind premium. Userpilot CSV exports are user-level only, no event streams.

**5/** 62% of orgs now use open source specifically to avoid vendor lock-in (Percona). The EU Data Act (effective Sept 2025) mandates machine-readable export formats. The regulatory direction is clear.
