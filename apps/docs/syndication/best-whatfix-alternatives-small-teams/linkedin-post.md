Whatfix costs the average customer $32,000 per year. Implementation takes 1-3 months.

For small SaaS teams, that budget could fund an entire engineering sprint on custom onboarding.

I compared 7 alternatives for teams with under 2,000 monthly active users. Key findings:

Product Fruits delivers the best cost per user at $0.053/MAU ($79/month). Userflow scores highest on G2 at 4.8/5 with 3,000 MAU for $240/month. And code-first libraries like Tour Kit eliminate per-user fees entirely.

The biggest surprise: Whatfix advertises a $299/month Starter plan, but real-world deployments for small teams start at $1,000-$2,000/month according to independent sources.
