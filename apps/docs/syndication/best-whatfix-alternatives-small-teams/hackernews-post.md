## Title: Whatfix alternatives for small teams: pricing comparison from $0 to $240/month

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/best-whatfix-alternatives-small-teams

## Comment to post immediately after:

I researched the actual cost-per-user of onboarding tools because Whatfix's "contact sales" approach makes it impossible to evaluate without a demo call.

The numbers: Whatfix averages $32K/year per customer. Even their advertised $299/month Starter plan reportedly starts at $1,000-$2,000/month in real deployments. For a 10-person SaaS startup, that's hard to justify.

I compared 7 alternatives across pricing, setup time, and G2 ratings. The range goes from $0 (open-source) to $279/month for SaaS tools. Product Fruits had the best per-user cost at $0.053/MAU. Userflow had the highest G2 rating at 4.8.

Disclosure: I built Tour Kit (one of the tools listed), so I'm biased. But the pricing data comes from published rate cards and independent sources (ITQlick, G2). I tried to be honest about every tool's limitations, including Tour Kit's (no visual builder, React-only, smaller community).
