# How to design onboarding for free trial vs freemium

Free trial and freemium aren't just pricing models. They're fundamentally different user psychology, and your onboarding flow needs to match. A trial user arrives with a deadline ticking in their head. A freemium user arrives with no commitment and no urgency.

Free trials convert at 15-25% (no credit card required). Freemium self-serve converts at 2-5%. But freemium CAC runs 50-60% lower. The gap between these numbers isn't pricing — it's onboarding design.

Key data points:
- Every 10-minute delay in time-to-first-value costs ~8% in conversion
- 7-14 day trials with urgency cues outperform 30-day trials by 71%
- Behavior-based emails during trials convert at 24% (8x generic emails)
- Best PLG companies reach "wow moment" in under 5 minutes
