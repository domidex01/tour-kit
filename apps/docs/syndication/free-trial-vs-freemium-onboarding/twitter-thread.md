**1/** Free trial and freemium aren't just pricing decisions. They need completely different onboarding engineering.

Trial users arrive with a deadline. Freemium users arrive with zero commitment.

Here's what the data says about designing for each:

**2/** Trial onboarding = sprint

- Hit activation in 30-40% of trial duration
- 7-14 day trials with urgency cues outperform 30-day by 71%
- Behavior-based emails convert at 24% (8x generic)
- Every 10-min delay in time-to-value costs ~8% in conversion

**3/** Freemium onboarding = marathon

- Gate value amplifiers, not basic utility
- Contextual hints > linear product tours
- 2-5% conversion rate, but 2-5x signup volume
- CAC runs 50-60% lower than trial

The math works differently. Design accordingly.

**4/** The real pattern: 65% of PLG SaaS now runs hybrid models (freemium + premium feature trials).

"Hybrid models are deceptively complex" — Ayush Agarwal, Dodo Payments

Dual entitlement states, conditional onboarding paths, two sets of upgrade prompts.

**5/** The gap nobody fills: WCAG-compliant onboarding.

- Accessible countdown timers (aria-live)
