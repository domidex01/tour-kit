Your onboarding flow is probably wrong for half your users.

Free trial users and freemium users have completely different psychology at signup. Trial users arrive in evaluation mode with a deadline. Freemium users arrive with zero commitment and no urgency. Yet most products run the same onboarding flow for both.

The data is clear: every 10-minute delay in time-to-first-value costs roughly 8% in conversion. 7-14 day trials with urgency cues outperform 30-day trials by 71%. And 65% of PLG SaaS companies now run hybrid models combining both approaches.

I wrote a guide breaking down the structural differences between trial and freemium onboarding, with React code examples for implementing each pattern. The most interesting finding: there's essentially zero guidance anywhere on making onboarding flows WCAG-accessible. Countdown timers, focus management, reduced-motion support — it's a gap across the entire industry.
