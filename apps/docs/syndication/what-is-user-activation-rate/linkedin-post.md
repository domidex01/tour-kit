The average SaaS activation rate is 36%.

Two out of three signups leave before experiencing what your team built. And most product tours aren't helping.

Chameleon analyzed 15 million product tour interactions and found something uncomfortable: click-through tours have decent completion rates (61%) but don't improve activation. Users click "Next" without doing anything meaningful.

What does work, based on the data:

- Action-based tours where users perform real behaviors: 123% higher completion
- Capping tours at 5 steps
- Onboarding checklists: up to 3x conversion in case studies
- Progress indicators: +12% completion, -20% dismissals

The revenue impact compounds. A 25% activation improvement correlates with 34% MRR growth over 12 months.
