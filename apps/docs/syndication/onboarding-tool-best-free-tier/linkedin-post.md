Most "free" onboarding tools aren't actually free.

I tested 10 SaaS onboarding tools and 8 open-source libraries to find which ones have genuine free-forever plans vs. trials that convert to $249+/month.

The results: only 6 out of 10 SaaS tools offer a real free plan. Product Fruits leads with 5,000 MAU. Most others cap at 1,000. And the jump from free to paid is steep -- Pendo goes from $0 to $15,000+/year.

The angle nobody covers: I reviewed 15 existing comparison articles. Zero evaluate WCAG accessibility compliance. Onboarding overlays and focus traps are among the most common accessibility failure points in modern web apps.

For engineering teams, open-source libraries (MIT license, no MAU limits) remain the strongest free option if you have developers who can implement them.
