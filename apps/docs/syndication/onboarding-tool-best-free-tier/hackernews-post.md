## Title: Which onboarding tool has the best free tier? (2026 comparison)

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/onboarding-tool-best-free-tier

## Comment to post immediately after:

I tested the "free" tier of 10 SaaS onboarding tools and 8 open-source libraries. The main finding: only 6 SaaS tools have genuine free-forever plans. The rest (Appcues, Userpilot, WalkMe, Whatfix) are trials that jump to $249-$10,000+/month.

Product Fruits has the highest free MAU cap at 5,000 users. Most others cap at 1,000 (Userflow, Chameleon, CommandBar) or 500 (Pendo). UserGuiding's free tier only covers 100 MAU.

The most surprising finding: I reviewed 15 competitor comparison articles and not a single one evaluates WCAG accessibility. Onboarding tours inject overlays, trap focus, and add dynamic content -- all patterns that break screen readers when implemented incorrectly.

I built Tour Kit (MIT, headless React library), so I have obvious bias here. I tried to be fair in the comparison -- every data point is verifiable against public pricing pages, npm, and GitHub. Happy to answer questions about the methodology or specific tools.
