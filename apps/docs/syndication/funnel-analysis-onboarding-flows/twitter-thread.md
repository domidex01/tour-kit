**1/** Most onboarding funnels track the wrong metric. Completion rate tells you if users watched the tour. Activation rate tells you if they got value. They're not the same thing.

**2/** 79% of funnel drop-off happens between signup and the first meaningful action. The fix isn't a longer tour. It's a shorter path to the core action your product exists for. TTV under 5 minutes = trial conversion above 25%.

**3/** Activation benchmarks vary wildly by industry:
- AI/ML tools: 54.8%
- B2B SaaS: 37.5%
- FinTech: 5%

Using a generic "aim for 40%" without industry context leads to wrong priorities.

**4/** A blind spot no one talks about: analytics tools can't detect screen reader users (intentionally, for privacy). Your funnel data is structurally incomplete for users on assistive tech. If your onboarding breaks for keyboard-only users, they drop off silently.

**5/** The improvement loop that actually works:
1. Find the biggest drop-off step
2. Watch session replays to understand WHY
3. Fix it and measure activation quality, not just completion
4. Repeat
