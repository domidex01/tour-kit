**1/** Your PM asks "what's the ROI of our product tours?"

Most teams freeze. The answer isn't one formula — it's four separate calculations. Here's the breakdown:

**2/** Formula 1: Activation Lift ROI

Average SaaS activation rate is 36% (Userpilot 2025). If your tour pushes that from 30% to 42%, that's 120 extra activated users per 1,000 signups.

At $600 ACV and 25% conversion: $18K/month in incremental revenue.

**3/** Formula 2: Churn Prevention

70% of SaaS customers churn within 90 days (UserGuiding 2026).

If tours reduce 90-day churn by 13 points, that's 130 users retained per monthly cohort. At $49/mo ARPU over 14 months: $89K per cohort.

**4/** Formula 3: Support Deflection

The easiest to calculate. Average support ticket costs $15-25 (Zendesk 2025).

If tours cut tickets on covered topics by 40%, multiply savings by volume. Finance teams love this one.

**5/** The biggest mistake: attributing 100% to the tour.

Tour completers are self-selected motivated users. Discount by 40-60% unless you're running a proper A/B test.

A "conservative" 1,357% ROI is more credible than an honest-looking 3,442%.
