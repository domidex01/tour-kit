75% of internet users speak a non-English language. Your product tour is the first thing they interact with — and it's probably broken in Arabic, Hebrew, and 40+ other languages.

I wrote a deep-dive on the i18n challenges nobody covers: how tooltip positioning breaks in RTL layouts, why "Step 1 of 5" needs ICU MessageFormat (Arabic has 6 plural categories), and the invisible problem of untranslated ARIA labels for screen reader users.

One finding that surprised me: the combined bundle cost of tour library + i18n library ranges from 8.5KB to 67KB depending on your choices. Nobody publishes these numbers.

Full guide with code examples and comparison tables: https://usertourkit.com/blog/i18n-product-tours-rtl-plurals
