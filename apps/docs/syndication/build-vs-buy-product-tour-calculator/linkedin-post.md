We calculated the 3-year total cost of ownership for three product tour approaches.

The results depend on one variable most teams overlook: MAU count.

Below 25K MAUs, SaaS tools (Appcues, Pendo) are genuinely cheaper. Above 25K, per-user pricing compounds and a headless library approach costs roughly half.

The numbers:
- DIY from scratch: $142,500 over 3 years
- Headless library: $48,000 over 3 years
- SaaS at 50K MAUs: $99,000+ over 3 years

IBM research shows maintenance consumes 50-75% of total software costs. Atlassian spent $3M over three years building onboarding internally — and still couldn't iterate fast enough.

The gap nobody talks about: every build-vs-buy article presents two options. But "headless library" is a distinct third path that gives teams code ownership without the full DIY maintenance burden.
