## Thread (6 tweets)

**1/** I tested the analytics dashboards of Appcues, Userpilot, and Pendo for two weeks.

The gap between "powerful analytics" on the marketing page and what you can actually query is wild.

**2/** Appcues: only tracks events inside Appcues-built flows. No funnels, no path analysis, no session replay, no retroactive data.

Need to know what users did after the tour? That's a Segment integration.

**3/** Pendo is the analytics powerhouse — autocapture, retroactive queries, AI insights.

But: $48K/year median contract (Vendr data), hourly data refreshes, and accuracy is a top-5 G2 complaint.

**4/** The real finding: 6 metric categories NONE of them track.

- Survey fatigue accumulation
- Cross-mechanism correlation
- Complete AARRR mapping (Referral + Revenue = invisible)
- Their own bundle size impact on your app

**5/** If you already run PostHog or Amplitude, consider this: 10 custom events from your onboarding code might give you more insight than a $48K analytics subscription layered on top.
