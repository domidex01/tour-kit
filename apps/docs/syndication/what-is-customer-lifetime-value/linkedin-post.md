76% of companies agree customer lifetime value matters.
Only 42% can calculate it.

Here's the formula and the lever most teams overlook.

CLV = (ARPU x Gross Margin) / Churn Rate

Churn sits in the denominator, which means halving it doubles your CLV. At $20K ARPA, going from 5% to 2.5% monthly churn takes CLV from $320K to $640K.

The biggest churn driver? Poor onboarding accounts for 23% of SaaS customer churn. Users who don't find value in the first week have a 90% churn probability.

Harvard Business Review makes an interesting point most CLV discussions skip: the formula doesn't capture how customers become more valuable over time through capability growth. Good onboarding builds the competence that drives expansion revenue, not just retention.

With customer acquisition costs up 40-60% since 2023, the retention side of CLV has never been more important.
