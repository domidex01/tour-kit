**1/** CLV = (ARPU x Margin) / Churn Rate

Because churn is in the denominator, halving it doubles your customer lifetime value.

That denominator is the most important thing in SaaS economics, and onboarding controls it.

**2/** The data: poor onboarding accounts for 23% of SaaS customer churn.

Users who don't find value in the first week? 90% churn probability.

The first 90 days set the CLV trajectory before any renewal conversation happens.

**3/** Interactive onboarding vs. static docs:
- 42% higher feature adoption
- 50% higher activation rates
- 67% completion rate (click-triggered tours)

A 25% increase in activation correlates with 34% revenue growth.

**4/** Here's what most CLV discussions miss (credit to HBR):

The formula doesn't capture how customers become MORE valuable over time.

Good onboarding doesn't just prevent churn. It builds the competence that drives expansion revenue.

**5/** At $20K ARPA with 80% margin:

5% churn → $320K CLV
2.5% churn → $640K CLV (+100%)
1.5% churn → $1.07M CLV (+233%)

With CAC up 40-60% since 2023, the retention side of this equation matters more than ever.
