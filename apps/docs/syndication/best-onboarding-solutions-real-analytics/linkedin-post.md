We tested the analytics in 5 onboarding tools. Most of them default to showing vanity metrics.

"Tours started" and "guide impressions" look great in a dashboard. But they don't predict retention. A tour that 12,000 people started but only 200 completed isn't working.

The metrics that actually matter: activation rate, feature adoption by cohort, time-to-value. Forrester data shows aligning to actionable metrics drives 32% revenue growth.

What we found across Pendo ($48K/year), Userpilot ($249/month), Appcues ($299/month), Chameleon, and Tour Kit (free, open source):

→ Chameleon's 15M interaction study: 3-step tours hit 72% completion, 5-step tours drop to 34%
→ Self-serve tours complete at 123% higher rates than forced ones
→ None of the SaaS tools let you fully own your analytics data
