## Title: Onboarding NPS: when to ask, how to calculate, and what to do with the score

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/onboarding-nps-survey

## Comment to post immediately after:

Most teams I've talked to treat onboarding NPS the same way: ship a 0-10 survey, check the dashboard once, forget about it. The number sits there while detractors churn and promoters never get asked for a referral.

I wrote up the full lifecycle approach we use — when to trigger the survey (24-72 hours after onboarding, not immediately), how to avoid the calculation gotchas (sample size under 30 makes the score meaningless), and what to actually do with each cohort.

The most useful finding: detractors who hear back from a human within 48 hours are 2.3x more likely to stay (Retently data). That single change had more impact than any survey question optimization.

Includes TypeScript code for the NPS calculation, benchmark tables for B2B SaaS (20-40 is typical, developer tools land 15-30), and a React implementation using Tour Kit's surveys package.
