**3/** Here's the part most pricing pages won't tell you:

Hosted platforms (Appcues, Pendo) run servers and databases for you. That infrastructure justifies recurring costs.

But a client-side JS library that ships in your bundle? No servers. Near-zero marginal cost.

The subscription is the business model, not the cost structure.

**4/** The lifetime deal trap is real too.

~40% of AppSumo products shut down within 3 years.

AppSumo takes 70% of revenue. A $160K launch nets the founder ~$48K while adding thousands of permanently-supported customers.

Perpetual license with version scoping > marketplace fire sale.

**5/** When subscriptions still make sense:

- You need a visual drag-and-drop builder
- Server-side user targeting
- Cross-device session persistence
- No developer on the team

Hosted platforms exist for a reason. The question is whether YOUR use case requires hosted infrastructure.

**6/** The framework for bootstrapped teams:

1. List features you actually need today (not the full vendor matrix)
2. Price out the subscription at 36 months + MAU overages
3. Compare one-time alternatives (include integration time)
4. Factor in switching costs
