# Gamification in onboarding: badges, streaks, and progress bars that work

Most SaaS onboarding flows have a completion problem. The median checklist completion rate sits at 10.1%, with an average of 19.2% across the industry. That means roughly 80% of your new users abandon setup before reaching the features they signed up for.

Gamification (progress bars, streaks, badges, and milestone rewards) is how products like Duolingo pushed retention from 12% to 55%. But a meta-analysis of 32 experiments found that progress bars *decreased* completion when early progress felt slow. The implementation details matter more than the pattern itself.

This guide covers the three gamification mechanics that actually move activation metrics with accessible React code, the psychology behind each, and honest guidance on when to skip gamification entirely.
