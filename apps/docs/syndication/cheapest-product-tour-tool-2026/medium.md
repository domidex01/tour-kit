# What is the cheapest product tour tool in 2026?

## I compared 14 tools by real annual cost. The price range will surprise you.

*Originally published at [usertourkit.com](https://usertourkit.com/blog/cheapest-product-tour-tool-2026)*

The short answer depends on what "cheap" means to your team. Open-source libraries cost $0 upfront but require engineering time. SaaS tools start at $39/month for basic overlays and climb past $15,000/year for analytics. Tour Kit sits in a category of its own: $99 one-time.

We built Tour Kit, so take that context into account. Every price below comes from public pricing pages as of April 2026.

## The full pricing breakdown

I tracked down pricing for 14 product tour tools. Here's the real annual cost for a product with 2,000 monthly active users:

At the bottom: Tour Kit at $99 total (one-time, not annual). Produktly at $192/year. Usetiful at $468/year.

In the middle: UserGuiding at $2,088/year. Userpilot and Appcues both at $2,988/year. Chameleon at $3,348/year.

At the top: Pendo at $15,900/year. WalkMe around $9,000/year. Whatfix around $32,000/year.

Tour Kit's year-3 cost is the same as year 1. Every SaaS tool multiplies.

## What "free" actually costs

Pendo's free plan caps at 500 MAU and removes all integrations. Cross 501 users and you're looking at $15,900/year overnight. Chameleon gives you 1,000 MAU for free, then charges $279/month.

Product Fruits is the exception with 5,000 MAU free. Open-source libraries like Shepherd.js are MIT-licensed and genuinely free, but you'll spend engineering hours building analytics and targeting.

Watch out for Intro.js: AGPL v3 means commercial use requires a paid license.

## The MAU trap

Every SaaS product tour tool scales pricing with your monthly active users. At 2,000 MAU, Userpilot charges $249/month. At 10,000 MAU, that jumps to $499/month. One UserGuiding customer reported saving $14,000 annually by switching from Pendo.

Tour Kit's $99 works for 500 users or 500,000.

## Which is cheapest for you?

**Non-technical teams:** Usetiful at $39/month or Product Fruits' free tier.

**React developers:** Tour Kit at $99 one-time. Ten composable packages. TypeScript-first.

**Zero budget, plenty of time:** Shepherd.js or Driver.js (MIT, free). Budget 20-40 hours for analytics and targeting.

**Enterprise compliance required:** Pendo or WalkMe. $15,000-$50,000/year.
