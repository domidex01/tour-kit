## Title: Comparing the real cost of 14 product tour tools (2026)

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/cheapest-product-tour-tool-2026

## Comment to post immediately after:

I've been building Tour Kit (a React product tour library) and wanted to understand the actual cost landscape for product tour tools in 2026. So I pulled pricing from every tool with public or semi-public pricing.

The range is striking: open-source libraries at $0, budget SaaS at $192-$468/year, mid-range at $2,000-$4,500/year, and enterprise tools at $15,000-$96,000/year.

Two patterns stood out. First, every SaaS tool uses MAU-based pricing, which means your costs scale with user growth. A product paying $249/month at 2,000 MAU will pay $499/month at 10,000 MAU. Second, the free tiers are designed as traps — Pendo's jumps from $0 (500 MAU) to $15,900/year at 501 MAU.

Full disclosure: I built Tour Kit ($99 one-time), so I have skin in the game. All competitor pricing comes from their public pages and third-party reviews with sources linked.
