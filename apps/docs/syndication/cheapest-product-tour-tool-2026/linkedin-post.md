I compared the real cost of 14 product tour tools in 2026. The range surprised me.

At the bottom: open-source libraries at $0 and Tour Kit at $99 one-time. In the middle: SaaS tools from $468 to $4,500/year. At the top: enterprise platforms at $15,000 to $96,000/year.

Two patterns stood out. Every SaaS tool uses MAU-based pricing, so your onboarding costs scale with your product's success. And free tiers are designed as traps — Pendo jumps from $0 (500 MAU) to $15,900/year the moment you cross 501 users.

Full disclosure: I built Tour Kit, so I'm biased on the $99 recommendation. But the competitor pricing comes from public pages and third-party reviews.
