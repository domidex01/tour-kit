## Title: 58% of companies use product-led growth, but most users never activate

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/what-is-product-led-growth

## Comment to post immediately after:

I wrote this because most PLG content is aimed at product managers and growth marketers, not developers who actually build the onboarding experience.

The stat that surprised me most: 68% of developers who abandon trials cite "too much setup time" as the main reason. Only 12% cite pricing. The bottleneck for PLG products isn't top-of-funnel — it's activation.

Average activation rates are ~40% for single-user products, ~20% for multi-user. AB Tasty moved their tour completion from 2% to 15% and trial-to-PQL conversion from 0% to 20% by redesigning their guided tour experience.

I build Tour Kit, an open-source React product tour library, so I'm biased toward the "tours fix activation" angle. But the PLG definition and comparison data (PLG vs. sales-led, PQL vs. MQL conversion rates) should be useful regardless of what tools you use.
