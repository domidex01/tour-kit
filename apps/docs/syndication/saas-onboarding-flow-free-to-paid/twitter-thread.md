**2/** Slack doesn't show you everything on day one.

After signup: create a channel, invite a teammate, send a message. That's it.

Workflows and Huddles surface later. Teams who send 2,000 messages in 30 days convert at 93%.

Pattern: Progressive disclosure.

**3/** Canva's entire onboarding exists for one metric: first export.

Drop users into a template. Only action needed: customize and export.

Users who export in session one are 4x more likely to return within 48 hours.

Pattern: Aha moment shortcut.

**4/** The most interesting pattern: Ahrefs' reverse trial.

Instead of free → paid, they give FULL access for 7 days, then downgrade.

Loss aversion converts 2-3x better than traditional free tiers.

Adopted by Toggl, Mixpanel, and Grammarly too.

**5/** The 3 mistakes that kill conversion:

1. Showing features instead of outcomes
2. Onboarding once and disappearing
3. Treating all free users the same

A user from Google search and a user invited by a teammate need different flows.
