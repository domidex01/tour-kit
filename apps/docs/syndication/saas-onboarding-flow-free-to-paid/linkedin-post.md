The average SaaS freemium conversion rate is 3-5%.

Some products consistently hit 8-10%. The difference isn't pricing. It's what happens in the first 72 hours after signup.

I analyzed onboarding flows from Slack, Notion, Figma, Dropbox, Canva, Loom, and Ahrefs. Found 7 distinct patterns:

Slack reveals features gradually (93% conversion for active teams). Canva shortcuts to a finished output in 3 minutes (4x return rate). Ahrefs gives full paid access for 7 days then downgrades (2-3x conversion via loss aversion).

The biggest takeaway: successful onboarding doesn't teach features. It gets users to the one action that predicts retention. Fast.
