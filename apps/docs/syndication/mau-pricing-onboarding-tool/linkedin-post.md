Onboarding tools bill by monthly active users. The more your product grows, the more you pay.

I pulled real pricing data from every major vendor (Appcues, Pendo, Userpilot, Chameleon, UserGuiding) to see what this actually costs at scale.

At 2,000 MAUs, prices range from $69 to $600/month. At 10,000 MAUs, even the cheapest option is $299/month. Pendo's median enterprise contract is $48,500/year (Vendr data, 530 real deals).

The structural problem: the vendor profits when users log in, not when they convert or generate revenue. Freemium products get hit hardest, paying to onboard users who may never pay.

The counterpoint is real: infrastructure costs scale with users, the model is simple, and at $10M ARR it's a small percentage. But for products with non-linear growth or freemium tiers, the math breaks fast.
