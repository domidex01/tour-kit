Userpilot's pricing page says $299/month. The actual cost for a growing SaaS team? A lot more than that.

We mapped Userpilot's real pricing from 1,000 to 10,000 MAU using Vendr's transaction data, G2 reviews, and their own feature comparison. Key findings:

At 2,001 MAU, pricing jumps from $299/mo to $799/mo. One user triggers a $6K/year increase. Above that threshold, pricing is completely opaque. Vendr shows annual spend ranging from $7,638 to $60,680.

The 3-year TCO for a team growing to 5K MAU comes to $38,000+ once you add plan-gated features (A/B testing, custom analytics), add-ons (session replays, CRM sync), and the mobile tooling gap (Userpilot is web-only).

Is it worth it? For non-technical teams that need a visual flow builder, yes. For developer-led teams writing React, the math doesn't work.
