Most SaaS teams paying for Userpilot already have PostHog or Amplitude for product analytics. They're maintaining two event pipelines for the same user data.

We just published a migration guide for engineering teams ready to swap Userpilot ($8,500/year average for the Growth plan) with an open-source stack: Tour Kit for onboarding UX + PostHog for analytics.

The migration runs both systems side-by-side — 3-5 hours for a typical setup, zero production downtime.

The honest tradeoff: you lose the visual flow builder. Every tour change goes through code. For engineering-led product teams, that's a feature. For PM-led teams, it's worth serious consideration before switching.
