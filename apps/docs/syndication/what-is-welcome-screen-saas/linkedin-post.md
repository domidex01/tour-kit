33% of SaaS companies don't have a welcome screen.

90% of users churn if they don't grasp your product's value in week one.

That's a gap worth closing.

I looked at how Slack, Notion, Pinterest, Asana, and Google Analytics 4 handle their welcome screens. Three patterns stood out:

Segmentation screens that ask 1-3 questions and personalize everything after (40% better retention).

Action-first screens that skip the survey and get users doing something immediately (Slack's approach boosted activation 25%).

Progressive screens with visible progress indicators (completion rates up 22%).

The catch: 72% of users abandon onboarding with too many steps. Two or three questions is the limit.
