The in-app guidance market ranges from $69/month to $405,000/year.

I tested 10 tools across three categories — headless libraries, no-code platforms, and enterprise DAPs — to figure out which ones actually earn their price.

Key findings:

→ Enterprise DAPs (WalkMe, Whatfix) cost $79K-$405K/yr but handle multi-app workflows nothing else covers. They take 4-12 weeks to deploy.

→ Mid-market platforms (Userpilot, Appcues, Userflow) run $240-$300/month with no-code builders. Userpilot has the best analytics. Appcues has the fastest setup.

→ No major vendor benchmarks their JavaScript bundle impact. SaaS platform scripts add 80-200KB. Headless libraries like Tour Kit ship at under 8KB.

→ AI is the defining trend in 2026. Userflow generates flows from plain language. CommandBar replaced traditional tours with an AI search bar. Pendo suggests steps from behavioral data.
