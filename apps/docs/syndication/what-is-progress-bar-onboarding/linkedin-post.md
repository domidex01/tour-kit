78% of SaaS products now use progress bars in onboarding. The completion rate boost is 20-30%.

But here's the part that surprised me: progress bars can actually decrease completion when the animation decelerates. A meta-analysis of 32 experiments confirmed this — users who saw a slowing bar abandoned more often than those who saw no progress at all.

The fix is counterintuitive. Start the animation slow and speed up toward the end. Users perceive the overall experience as faster even when the total time is identical.
