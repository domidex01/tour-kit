I wrote a guide for engineering leads who want to pitch their CTO on replacing SaaS onboarding tools (Appcues, Pendo, WalkMe) with open-source React libraries. It includes the three-year TCO math ($115K-$160K+ SaaS vs $5K-$20K library), seven common CTO objections with data-backed responses, and a one-page pitch template.

The angle that worked best: SaaS onboarding tools inject third-party JS from an external CDN, creating compliance surface area that SOC 2 and HIPAA audits are increasingly flagging. The security argument resonated more than the cost argument for regulated teams.
