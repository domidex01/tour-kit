70% of new customer acquisitions fail in the first two months of onboarding. A/B testing your onboarding flows should help, but not all tools deliver what they promise.

I tested 7 onboarding tools with built-in experimentation features: Appcues, Userpilot, Pendo, Chameleon, Statsig, Firebase, and Tour Kit. The differences are significant.

Some findings: Pendo caps experiments at 2 variants. Appcues still lists control groups as "coming soon." Firebase just launched web A/B testing in March 2026 (free). And the biggest gap: not a single tool verifies WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance for experiment variants.

The $840M A/B testing tools market is growing at 11.5% CAGR, but the developer experience and accessibility standards haven't kept pace with the marketing.
