Intercom product tours cost $273/month minimum. The tours are linear-only, don't work on mobile, and their own data shows a 34% median completion rate.

I tested 6 alternatives in the same React 19 project and compared pricing, accessibility, and bundle size.

The pricing range: from $9.99 one-time (Intro.js) to $279/month (Chameleon). Most dedicated tools include tours as a core feature rather than charging for an add-on.

The gap nobody talks about: accessibility. Intercom achieved WCAG 2.0 AA for the Messenger widget but there's zero evidence of compliance for Product Tours specifically.

Full comparison with real numbers: https://usertourkit.com/blog/best-intercom-product-tour-alternatives
