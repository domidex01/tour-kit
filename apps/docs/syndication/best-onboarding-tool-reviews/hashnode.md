# Onboarding tools ranked by customer reviews (G2 + Capterra data)

According to [Userpilot's research](https://userpilot.com/blog/open-source-user-onboarding/), 63% of customers say onboarding quality is the deciding factor when they subscribe. That number explains why [Capterra lists 707 onboarding products](https://www.capterra.com/onboarding-software/) with over 14,000 published reviews. But here's the problem: most of those 707 products are HR/employee onboarding tools. The product tour and in-app guidance category sits under G2's "Digital Adoption Platform" label, and that's where the ratings actually matter for developers.

We pulled G2 and Capterra scores for the 10 tools that product and engineering teams actually evaluate. We installed the open-source libraries, tested the SaaS platforms, and cross-referenced review patterns to surface what the star ratings don't tell you.

Disclosure: Tour Kit is our project. We've tried to be fair. Every claim below is verifiable against G2, Capterra, GitHub, and npm.

## Key findings

**UserGuiding tops G2 at 4.7/5.** Product Fruits leads Capterra at 4.8/5. Chameleon has the strongest consistency with five consecutive G2 Leader badges.

**Open-source tools are invisible on review platforms.** Shepherd.js (13K GitHub stars), React Joyride (7.6K stars), and Driver.js have no G2 or Capterra profiles. The platforms most PMs use to evaluate tools completely exclude what most developers actually install.

**React Joyride is stuck on React 18.** Last stable release was November 2024. No React 19 support as of April 2026.

**Accessibility is the biggest gap.** Not a single tool in the G2 Digital Adoption Platform category advertises WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. We searched. Zero results.

**Pricing transparency varies wildly.** UserGuiding publishes pricing ($69/month). Appcues, Userpilot, Pendo, and Chameleon all hide behind "contact sales." The Capterra average entry price across 707 onboarding products is $79/month.

## How to choose

- **Headless library (Tour Kit):** React developers with custom design systems
- **Opinionated OSS (Shepherd.js, React Joyride):** Working tour in under an hour
- **SaaS platform (Appcues, Userpilot, UserGuiding):** Product teams, no engineering tickets
- **Enterprise DAP (Pendo, WalkMe):** Product analytics bundled with guidance
