## Title: Enterprise Product Tour Tools Compared: From $0 Open Source to $405K/Year Contracts

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/best-product-tour-tools-b2b-saas

## Comment to post immediately after:

I tested 8 product tour tools on enterprise-specific criteria: SSO, SOC 2 compliance, analytics depth, pricing transparency, and accessibility.

The pricing spread is remarkable. WalkMe averages $78K/year with contracts reaching $405K. Pendo ranges from free (500 MAU) to $142K/year. Mid-market tools like Userpilot and Appcues sit at $3K-$10K/year but gate SSO behind enterprise tiers.

The most interesting finding: none of the major platforms prominently document WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for their tour overlays. For B2B SaaS serving regulated industries, this is a procurement risk that rarely surfaces during vendor evaluation.

Disclosure: I work on Tour Kit (MIT open-source, headless React approach). The comparison tries to be fair — we acknowledge our limitations (no visual builder, smaller community, React-only). All pricing data is verifiable against vendor docs.
