Enterprise product tour tools range from $0 open-source to $405K/year contracts. Most teams sign without comparing.

- WalkMe averages $78K/year. Pendo ranges $15K-$142K. Mid-market tools gate SSO behind enterprise tiers
- 4-step tours hit 60.1% completion. 5+ steps drops below 20%
- No major platform prominently documents WCAG 2.1 AA compliance for their overlays

The real question isn't "which tool has more features." It's who builds the tours at your company: engineers, product managers, or L&D teams.
