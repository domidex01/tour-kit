The median Pendo contract costs $48,463/year. Appcues starts at $879/month for 2,500 users. Open-source tour libraries cost $0.

I compared all three approaches for teams evaluating product tour and onboarding tools.

The decision comes down to three questions: who edits tours (PMs or developers), what's the annual budget, and whether 200-300KB of injected JavaScript impacts your performance targets.

Enterprise SaaS tools give you visual builders and analytics dashboards. Open-source libraries give you 8KB bundles, full design system control, and predictable costs that don't scale with your user count.

Neither approach is universally better. It depends on your team structure and deploy cadence.
