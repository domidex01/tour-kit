# Retention Analytics for Onboarding: Measuring Week 1, Week 4, and Week 12

Most product teams track onboarding tour completion rates. Fewer teams ask the harder question: does completing the tour actually change retention?

Retention analytics for onboarding measures the percentage of users who continue engaging with your product at specific time intervals after their onboarding experience. The standard measurement windows are week 1 (7 days), week 4 (28 days), and week 12 (84 days), each representing a different phase of user commitment.

## Key Benchmarks

According to Mixpanel's 2025 Product Benchmarks, the median SaaS product retains 35% of users at week 1 and just 15% at week 12. Products with structured onboarding flows retain roughly 2x more users at every checkpoint (Appcues 2024).

The most actionable metric: the retention delta between users who completed onboarding versus those who skipped it. This isolates the tour's actual impact from self-selection bias.
