**3/** The most overlooked metric: completer vs skipper retention.

If you only measure overall retention, you're seeing selection bias. Motivated users complete tours AND retain better.

The real signal = completer retention minus skipper retention. That delta is your tour's actual lift.

**4/** Developer tools have higher week 1 retention than general B2B SaaS (45% vs 35%) but a steeper week 4 drop.

There's an "integration cliff" where devs decide: commit to the learning curve, or switch.

Week 4 is where you win or lose developer users.

**5/** The fix for each checkpoint:

Week 1 low? Shorten your tour. 4 steps > 8 steps (22% higher day-2 return).

Week 4 low? Build a "second onboarding" for advanced features.

Week 12 low? Map tours to expansion moments, not calendar dates.
