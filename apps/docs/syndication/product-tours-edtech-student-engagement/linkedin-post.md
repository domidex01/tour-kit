EdTech onboarding completion rates average 15.9%.

That's below the cross-industry average of 19.2%, and far from the 40-60% range that healthy SaaS products target.

The root cause: education platforms serve students, instructors, and administrators through the same interface — but show everyone the same onboarding tour. A student looking for their first assignment doesn't need a tour of the admin settings panel.

Three patterns that actually move the numbers:

1. Role-based tour routing — one question at login, completely different onboarding paths
2. Contextual hints instead of front-loaded walkthroughs — 50% higher activation rates
3. Semester-aware fatigue prevention — returning students see "what's new," not the same day-one tour

One compliance note: starting April 24, 2026, all public educational institutions must meet WCAG 2.1 AA for web content, including product tours and modal overlays. Accessible onboarding is now a legal requirement.
