# 8 best self-hosted onboarding tools in 2026

Every SaaS onboarding tool you've heard of sends user behavior data to someone else's servers. Step completions, click coordinates, session recordings, feature adoption metrics. That data lands in AWS us-east-1 or GCP us-central1 by default, regardless of where your users are.

For EU companies operating under GDPR, NIS2, and DORA, that's not just a compliance checkbox. Meta paid EUR 1.2 billion in May 2023 for transferring EU user data to the US. Uber got fined EUR 290 million in August 2024 for the same thing.

Self-hosted onboarding tools solve this. Some are client-side libraries where no data ever leaves the browser. Others are Docker-deployable platforms you run on your own infrastructure.
