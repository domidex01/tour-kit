# 8 Self-Hosted Onboarding Tools for Teams That Care About Data Sovereignty

*Your SaaS onboarding tool is probably sending user behavior data to US servers. Here are 8 alternatives you can run yourself.*

*Originally published at [usertourkit.com](https://usertourkit.com/blog/best-self-hosted-onboarding-tools)*

Every SaaS onboarding tool you've heard of sends user behavior data to someone else's servers. Step completions, click coordinates, session recordings, feature adoption metrics. That data lands in AWS us-east-1 by default.

For EU companies under GDPR, NIS2, and DORA, that's a real problem. Meta paid EUR 1.2 billion in May 2023 for transferring EU user data to the US. Uber got fined EUR 290 million in August 2024 for the same thing.

Self-hosted onboarding tools solve this. We tested 8 of them across two categories: client-side libraries (where no data ever leaves the browser) and Docker-deployable platforms (where data stays on your infrastructure).

The full article with a comparison table, detailed reviews of all 8 tools, and a decision framework is at usertourkit.com/blog/best-self-hosted-onboarding-tools.
