Other findings that changed how I think about onboarding:
- Click-triggered tours complete at 67% vs. 31% for auto-triggered ones
- 76.3% of static tooltips get dismissed within 3 seconds
- Personalization by user role lifts 7-day retention by 35%
- A visible skip button makes users MORE likely to engage, not less
