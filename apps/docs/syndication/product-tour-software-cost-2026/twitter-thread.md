## Thread (6 tweets)

**1/** Product tour software costs between $0 and $142,000/year in 2026.

I pulled pricing from 10 vendors. The gap between sticker price and actual cost is wild.

**2/** SaaS entry prices look reasonable:
- UserGuiding: $69/mo
- Userpilot: $249/mo
- Appcues: $300/mo

But they all price by MAU. At 20K MAUs, Appcues alone runs ~$2K/month. That's $24K/year for tooltip rendering.

**3/** 7 out of 8 SaaS tools switch to "Contact Sales" above a certain MAU count.

Hidden costs (implementation, compliance add-ons, training) push actual spend 2-5x beyond published prices.

**4/** Enterprise DAPs are another world entirely:
- Pendo: $15K-$142K/year
- WalkMe: $9K-$50K/year

You're buying the analytics suite and support contract as much as the tour functionality.

**5/** Open-source alternative: $0 licensing + 8-40 hours of dev time.

For a 5K MAU product over 3 years, the difference vs SaaS is $14K-$53K.

Trade-off: no visual builder, your engineers own it.
