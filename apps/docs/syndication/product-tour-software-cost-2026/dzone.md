# Product tour software pricing in 2026: a full breakdown for engineering teams

Product tour software costs between $0 and $142,000 per year in 2026, depending on whether you choose SaaS platforms, enterprise digital adoption platforms, or open-source libraries. This article compares pricing across 11 tools with real costs at 5,000 and 10,000 monthly active users.

## Key pricing data

- SaaS platforms (Appcues, Userpilot, Chameleon): $69-$300/month entry, scaling with MAU count
- Enterprise DAPs (Pendo, WalkMe): $9,000-$142,000/year
- Open-source libraries (React Joyride, Shepherd.js, Tour Kit): $0 licensing, 8-40 hours integration time

## Hidden cost analysis

A CompareTiers analysis found SaaS hidden costs push actual spend 2-5x beyond published prices. The four hidden cost categories: MAU overages, implementation/training ($2K-$50K), compliance add-ons (enterprise-gated), and switching costs (proprietary data formats).

## Decision framework

The right pricing model depends on team composition, MAU growth rate, design system maturity, and compliance requirements. For React teams, open-source libraries deliver the best three-year TCO. For product-led teams without frontend engineers, SaaS visual builders justify the subscription.
