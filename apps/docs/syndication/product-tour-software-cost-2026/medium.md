# How Much Does Product Tour Software Actually Cost in 2026?

### The pricing gap between sticker price and real cost is wider than you think

*Originally published at [usertourkit.com](https://usertourkit.com/blog/product-tour-software-cost-2026)*

You're evaluating product tour tools and every vendor pricing page gives you the same thing: a grid of features, a monthly number with an asterisk, and a "Talk to Sales" button for the plan you actually need. None of them show you what the bill looks like after your MAU count doubles.

We pulled pricing data from 10 vendor websites, cross-referenced with published G2 and Capterra reviews, and calculated what each option actually costs over 12 months for a 5,000 MAU SaaS product.

**Disclosure:** we built Tour Kit, so we're biased toward the open-source path. Every number below is sourced and verifiable.

---

## The short answer

Product tour software costs between $0 and $142,000 per year in 2026. That's not a typo. SaaS platforms like Appcues and Userpilot charge $249-$300/month for entry plans with 1,000-2,000 monthly active users, scaling to $750+ as you grow. Enterprise digital adoption platforms (Pendo, WalkMe) run $9,000 to $142,000 annually.

Open-source libraries like Tour Kit, React Joyride, and Shepherd.js cost $0 in licensing, with the real expense being developer time: typically 8-40 hours depending on complexity.

---

## The hidden costs nobody talks about

The subscription fee is the beginning, not the total. Four categories of hidden costs don't appear on any pricing page:

**MAU overages** are the biggest surprise. Appcues at 1,000 MAUs costs $300/month. At 20,000 MAUs, published G2 quotes put it north of $2,000/month. That's $24,000/year for tooltip rendering.

**Implementation and training** add $2,000 to $50,000 depending on complexity. Staff training averages 20-30 hours per admin.

**Compliance gates** (SSO, audit logs, SOC 2) are almost always locked behind the enterprise tier.

**Switching costs** are the trap door. No SaaS onboarding tool offers clean data export. When you switch, you rebuild from zero.

---

## The decision framework

- **Have React developers + design system?** Use open-source. 8-40 hours setup, zero recurring fees.
- **Product-led team without frontend engineers?** SaaS tools make sense. Budget $300-$600/month.
- **Enterprise with 50K+ users?** Pendo or WalkMe. Budget $25K-$100K/year.
- **Startup watching cash?** If you have one React developer, open-source pays for itself in 2-3 months.

The product tour software market was valued at $478 million in 2025 and is projected to reach $781 million by 2031. Prices are going up, not down.
