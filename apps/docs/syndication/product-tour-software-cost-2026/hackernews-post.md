## Title: Product tour software costs $0 to $142k/yr in 2026 – a pricing breakdown of 10 tools

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/product-tour-software-cost-2026

## Comment to post immediately after:

I maintain an open-source product tour library (Tour Kit), so I'm biased here — disclosing that upfront.

I pulled pricing data from 10 product tour vendors (Appcues, Userpilot, Chameleon, UserGuiding, Userflow, Intercom, Pendo, WalkMe) plus the three main open-source options (React Joyride, Shepherd.js, Tour Kit) and tried to calculate what each one actually costs for a 5,000 MAU SaaS product over 12 months.

The most interesting finding: 7 out of 8 SaaS tools switch to "Contact Sales" above a certain MAU threshold. The sticker prices ($69-$300/month) are for the smallest plans. A CompareTiers analysis found hidden costs (implementation, compliance add-ons, MAU overages) push actual spend 2-5x beyond published prices.

The open-source path costs $0-$99 in licensing but 8-40 hours of developer time. Whether that trade-off makes sense depends entirely on whether you have React developers on the team. The product tour software market was valued at $478M in 2025 — prices are going up across the board.

Happy to answer questions about methodology or specific vendor pricing.
