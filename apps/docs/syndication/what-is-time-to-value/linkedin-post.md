78% onboarding completion rate. 50% churn within 30 days.

The metric most product teams watch (completion rate) doesn't predict retention. The one that does: Time to Value.

I compiled benchmark data from 547 SaaS companies. The average TTV is 1 day, 12 hours. But the variance is striking:

- CRM tools: ~28 hours
- HR Tech: ~3.8 days  
- Top PLG products targeting 2026: under 60 seconds

Most articles list 3-4 types of TTV. There are actually 7. The two most overlooked: perceived TTV (UX makes the same duration feel shorter) and recurring TTV (each use cycle has its own value moment).

Improving activation rate by 25% drives a 34% revenue boost. The four strategies that move TTV: remove signup friction, guide users to the value event, progressive disclosure, and AI-personalized onboarding paths.
