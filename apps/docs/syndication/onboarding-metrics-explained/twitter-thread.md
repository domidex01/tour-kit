**2/** USER ACTIVATION RATE

Formula: (Users who completed activation event / Total new signups) x 100

Benchmarks:
- Poor: <20%
- Average: 20-40%
- Good: 40-60%
- Excellent: 60%+

This is the #1 metric. If users don't activate, nothing else matters.

**3/** TIME TO VALUE

Formula: Timestamp of activation event - Timestamp of first login

Average across 547 SaaS companies: 1 day, 12 hours, 23 minutes.

Teams that measure TTV instead of completion rate see 15-25% higher trial-to-paid conversion.

**4/** The metric nobody tracks but should: CUSTOMER EFFORT SCORE (CES)

"How easy was it to [complete this task]?" on a 1-7 scale.

Stronger predictor of loyalty than NPS or CSAT (Gartner).

Above 5.0 = low friction. Below 4.0 = problem.

**5/** RETENTION CURVES tell you more than single-point metrics:

- Week 1 retention (median SaaS): 35%
- Week 4: 20%
- Week 12: 15%

With structured onboarding: 2.6x higher at week 4.

The shape of the curve matters more than any single number.

**6/** Which metrics to prioritize by stage:

Pre-PMF: Activation rate + TTV only
Post-PMF: Add retention curves + trial-to-paid
At scale: Layer in DAU/MAU, CES, ROI

Tracking more than 8 metrics = dashboard overload with zero action.
