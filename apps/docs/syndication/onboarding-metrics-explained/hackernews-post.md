## Title: Every onboarding KPI explained with formulas and benchmark data

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/onboarding-metrics-explained

## Comment to post immediately after:

I put together a reference guide covering every onboarding metric worth tracking: activation rate, time to value, DAU/MAU, NPS, CSAT, CES, funnel analysis, retention curves, and ROI.

Each metric includes the formula and benchmark data. A few data points that stood out during research: the average SaaS activation rate is 36% (Userpilot), average tour completion is 61% across 15M interactions (Chameleon), and products with structured onboarding retain 2.6x more users at week 4 (Appcues). CES is a stronger loyalty predictor than NPS but barely anyone tracks it during onboarding.

The guide also includes a decision framework for which metrics to prioritize by company stage. Pre-PMF teams should focus on activation rate and TTV. Post-PMF adds retention curves and trial-to-paid. At scale, layer in DAU/MAU and ROI.

Disclosure: I built User Tour Kit, an open-source product tour library for React. The guide references it for instrumentation examples but the metrics and benchmarks are tool-agnostic.
