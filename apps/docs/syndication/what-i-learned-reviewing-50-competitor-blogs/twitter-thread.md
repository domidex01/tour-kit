Appcues, Userpilot, Pendo, WalkMe, Whatfix, and a dozen others. Hundreds of articles.

**2/** Every vendor runs the EXACT same playbook:

- "X vs Y" comparisons where they win
- "Best tools" listicles where they're #1
- Feature guides that funnel into demos

The top 10 vendors have published 200+ comparison posts. Everyone claims to be "honest." Nobody is.

**3/** The biggest blind spot: nobody writes for developers.

Every blog targets PMs and marketers. I searched 50 blogs for actual code examples. Almost nothing.

Meanwhile DigitalOcean has 8,000+ developer tutorials. That's the model that works.

**4/** AI content is everywhere -- and already declining.

85% of tech marketers use AI (CMI 2026). But AI drafting usage dropped from 57% to 44% this year.

The tell: perfectly parallel paragraphs, synonym cycling, "In today's rapidly evolving..." throat-clearing. You can spot it instantly when you read 50 blogs in a row.

**5/** Every "product tour examples" post references the same 5 companies: Slack, Shopify, Asana, Notion, Figma.

70% overlap across competing listicles.

Original examples? Original data? Almost nonexistent.
