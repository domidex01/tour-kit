## Title: Digital adoption platforms for startups: pricing from $55/mo to $7K/yr

## URL: https://usertourkit.com/blog/best-digital-adoption-platforms-startups

## Comment to post immediately after:

I've been building a product tour library (Tour Kit) and kept running into the same question from small teams: "Which DAP should we use?" Most comparisons are written for enterprise buyers with $50K+ budgets.

So I tested 7 platforms at startup scale — installed each one, built the same onboarding flow (welcome tour, checklist, NPS survey), and compared real pricing.

The range is wide: HelpHero at $55/mo, UserGuiding at $89/mo, Hopscotch at $99/mo, Appcues at $249/mo. Pendo has a free tier (500 MAU) but jumps to ~$7K/year once you outgrow it. On the open-source side, Shepherd.js is free but tours-only.

The gap that surprised me most: none of the DAP comparison articles I found discuss bundle size, accessibility compliance, or what happens to your onboarding flows when you want to switch providers. Enterprise buyers don't care about those things. Startups should.

Disclosure: Tour Kit is my project. I tried to be fair — every claim is verifiable against npm, GitHub, and public pricing pages.
