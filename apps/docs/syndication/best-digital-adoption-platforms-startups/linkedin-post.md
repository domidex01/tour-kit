The digital adoption platform market hit $1 billion in 2024.

But if you're running a startup with 12 people and a tight budget, that market isn't built for you.

I tested 7 DAPs at startup scale. The pricing range tells the story:

→ HelpHero: $55/mo
→ UserGuiding: $89/mo  
→ Hopscotch: $99/mo
→ Appcues: $249/mo
→ Pendo: Free for 500 users, then $7K/year

Most platforms gate surveys, checklists, and analytics behind their $200+ tiers. Pre-Series A teams end up paying enterprise prices for startup-scale usage.

The decision comes down to two questions:
1. Do you have React developers on the team?
2. What's your monthly budget for tooling?

If you have devs: use an npm library. Smaller bundles, no per-MAU pricing, full design system control.

If you don't: HelpHero or UserGuiding under $100/mo gets you running without engineering time.
