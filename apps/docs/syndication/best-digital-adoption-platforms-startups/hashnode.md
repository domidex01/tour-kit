# 7 best digital adoption platforms for startups in 2026

The digital adoption platform market hit $1 billion in 2024 and is headed toward $4.8 billion by 2033. But most of that growth is enterprise money chasing enterprise tools. WalkMe got acquired by SAP for $1.5 billion. Pendo's paid plans start at $7,000 a year.

None of that helps a 12-person startup trying to reduce churn.

We tested seven digital adoption platforms by building the same onboarding flow: a welcome tour, a feature checklist, and an NPS survey. We measured pricing at startup scale, checked bundle impact, and noted where each tool either nailed or fumbled the small-team use case.

*We built Tour Kit, so take our #1 ranking with appropriate skepticism. Every claim below is verifiable against npm, GitHub, bundlephobia, and public pricing pages.*

## 1. Tour Kit — best for React teams who want full control

Tour Kit is a headless digital adoption toolkit for React. Instead of injecting a third-party script, you install npm packages and render onboarding UI with your own components. The core ships at under 8 KB gzipped with zero runtime dependencies.

**Strengths:** Core under 8 KB gzipped. TypeScript-first. WCAG 2.1 AA compliant. One-time $99 Pro license.

**Limitations:** Requires React developers. No visual builder. React 18+ only.

## 2. HelpHero — cheapest full-featured SaaS

$55/month for tours, hotspots, and checklists. No surveys. Limited analytics. Best for seed-stage budgets.

## 3. UserGuiding — fastest no-code setup

$89/month with tours, checklists, resource centers, and NPS. We had a tour running in 20 minutes.

## 4. Hopscotch — most transparent pricing

$99/month for 3,000 MAU. Every plan and price is public. No sales calls required.

## 5. Shepherd.js — best open-source for simple tours

Free (MIT). 12,000+ GitHub stars. Tours only, no checklists or surveys. Works with any framework.

## 6. Appcues — best no-code builder

$249/month. Best visual flow builder. 45+ integrations. Steep for pre-Series A.

## 7. Pendo Free — analytics-first bootstrapped teams

Free tier with analytics, guides, and NPS for 500 MAU. Paid plans start at ~$7,000/year.

## How to choose

- **Developer library (Tour Kit, Shepherd.js):** Code-level control, smaller bundles, no per-MAU pricing
- **Budget SaaS (HelpHero, Hopscotch, UserGuiding):** No engineering bandwidth required
- **Full-stack SaaS (Appcues, Pendo):** Visual builder for product managers
